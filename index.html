Welcome to

FLYCHEESE STUDIO

Making expression accessible

We are a Digital Art Training studio focused on empowering individuals with Autism or who require additional support, to learn and develop their digital art skills.


Through our Workshops and accredited courses, we provide accessible learning opportunities that cater to a diverse range of needs and aspirations.


Our specialization lies in animation and digital art, offering a wide array of creative opportunities across various mediums. As part of our commitment to inclusion, we provide students with real-world experience by involving them in Professional Projects


Many of our students have advanced to become valued members of our team, showcasing the potential for growth and professional development within our supportive environment.
